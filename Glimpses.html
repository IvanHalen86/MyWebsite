As a science communicator, I co-organized "Glimpses" an exhibition to bridge the gap between art and science. On one hand, science opens up vast possibilities for experimentation with materials and techniques that provide fresh art perspectives. On the other hand, art offers an engaging narrative that humanizes science and facilitates communication. With Glimpses, we wanted to allow NYUAD scientific Departments to exhibit their research artistically, promoting the synergy between art and science for innovation and creativity.

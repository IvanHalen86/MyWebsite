As science communicator, I co-organized "Glimpses", an exhibition that aimed to bridge the gap between art and science. Science opens up vast possibilities for experimentation with materials and techniques that may provide fresh art perspectives. Art, on the other hand, provides the engaging narrative that humanizes science and facilitates its communication. With Glimpses we wanted to give an opportunity to NYUAD scientific Departments to exhibit their research in an artistic way promoting the synergy between art and science for innovation and creativity.

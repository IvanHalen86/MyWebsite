Motion Capture

This is an example of motion capture analysis I did for an experiment where I investigated the action anticipation ability in basketball players. I recorded the sound and the movement (stick figure) of a professional basketball player while performing a deceptive action (video on the left). I then tested the ability of a basketball players' group in catching the attacker only by using the sound the attacker made during the movement (video on the right). Link to the paper
By recording the attacker's and participant's Center of Mass movements trhough a motion capture system I defined when the attacker (blue line) and the participant (red line) moved and calculate the delay between the two movements.

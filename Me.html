I am a Research Associate at the New York University Abu Dhabi (NYUAD), and I am strongly interested in motor control and multisensory integration. Specifically, my work investigates the tight link between auditory, visual, and haptic (proprioceptive and tactile) sensory information and motor control through multiple techniques.

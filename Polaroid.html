Beside my research activity, I like very much Polaroid photography. I own several of them (about 10), from Land cameras to Polaroid 600s, SX70, and spectra. Here there are some pictures taken with a modified Polaroid Land 320. I modified the battery compartment to fit two batteries of, respectively, 1.5 and 2 volts.
However, it's now impossible to find Polaroid Land camera films, so I switch to the more modern Fujifilm Instax Wide 300 whose films are still in production.
